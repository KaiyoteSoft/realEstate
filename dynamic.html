This is a mockup of a real estate site using media from a RICOH THETA S 360 degree camera. The site is based on a concept described in the THETA Developers Unofficial Guide. In the concept, real estate agents are sent THETA S cameras and a special smartphone application that is used to take 360 pictures and send them to a central site. You can get started building the smartphone app by looking at the RICOH THETA Official SDK. The viewer is based on code written by Atsushi Kokubo.
